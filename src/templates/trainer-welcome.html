WELCOME to NETQWIX!

NetQwix is here to modernize the way you teach and correspond with customers worldwide, affectionately known as the NetQwix Enthusiast.

NetQwix puts your customers right at your fingertips LIVE - anytime, anywhere right from your smartphone or computer.  Better convenience means more sessions - and more sessions benefit everyone.  With NetQwix, no season is the off season - we help you turn one-time clients into regulars regardless of where your professional journey takes you.  

 

It's easy to use NetQwix.  Here are a few tips to guide you on getting started.  You can also call NetQwix support at 212-829-8749 if you have any issues.

Setting your Session Fee:

You earn 80% of the session fee.

Setting your NETQWIX Schedule:

  • Click Schedule and then select the times you would like to be available each day 

  • Use the repeat icon if you would like to make your schedule the same every day  

  • Enthusiasts can request an Instant Session any time and we will alert you via text

  • Accept the Session via text and you the link will bring you right into the session

Confirming Sessions:

Hit "Confirm" when you get NetQwix’s Session Request & the enthusiast will be alerted.

Uploading Footage:

You can upload video clips from your phone which can be referenced during the session using NetQwix's Clip Analysis Mode on the fly during the live session experience.  

Ending the Session:

  • The session ends when the timer concludes
  • NetQwix leaves 2 minutes of extra time for you give last minute tips and plan for your next session

Finish Game Plan for Enthusiast:

  • You can edit tips after the session.  Click "Update" and the changes will save for the student also
  • Enthusiasts will be able to print the game plans at the end of the session - please make the tips clear 

NetQwix - How it Works:

1. Expert or Enthusiast records swings while playing or practicing.

2. Enthusiast joins NetQwix and uploads and labels video clips of pre-recorded footage in any format.

3. Enthusiast searches for their favorite Expert and requests a session.  

4. Sessions can be booked in advance or Experts can be booked instantly – in which case NetQwix will contact the Expert for immediate confirmation.

5. When the Expert accepts the Instant or Advanced session, the Enthusiast is asked to purchase the lesson.  

6. Payment will prompt NetQwix to provide a unique meeting link where the Expert and Enthusiast will be magically connected inside NetQwix’s custom Live Session portal.

7. NetQwix’s system enables the Expert to diagram and analyze selected footage live while the Enthusiast watches and they discuss.  The system also uses the phone cam/webcam and microphone so changes can be implemented by the Enthusiast under the watchful eye of their Expert, with the Session alternating between the two experiences, which is controlled live by the expert.

8. The Sessions, as well as clips of all footage uploaded by the enthusiast, are available for review in their personalized locker.

9. A printable Game Plan reflecting the Session experience will be generated by the Expert and placed in the Enthusiast’s locker for future reference.

In summary, Enthusiasts will receive a ‘Locker’ from which they can play uploaded swings, watch a recording of their lessons, and view each Game Plan generated.
